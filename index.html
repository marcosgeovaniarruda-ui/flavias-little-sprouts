About Us

Meet Flávia

My love for caring for children began within my own family, with the arrival of my grandchildren. Being part of their growth, giving them love, attention, and care made me realize how special this calling truly is. And little by little, I began to wonder: why not extend this love and care to other families as well?

Over the years, I had the opportunity to work in daycare settings and also care for children within wonderful families, and each experience only made my passion grow stronger. Every child I met touched my heart in a unique way and confirmed what I already felt deep inside — caring for children is what I truly love to do.

Flavia's Little Sprouts was born from this love: a warm, family-centered space created with affection, patience, kindness, and genuine care. My goal is for every child to feel safe, welcomed, loved, and happy here — just like family.

FAQ

Common Questions

Find answers to frequently asked questions about our daycare.

We accept children from 4 months to 3 years old. We welcome infants and toddlers and provide age-appropriate care for each stage of development.
We are open Monday through Friday, from 7:30 AM to 5:30 PM. We are closed on major holidays—parents are notified in advance.
We provide a morning snack, a nutritious homemade lunch, and an afternoon snack. All meals are prepared fresh on-site. Please inform us of any dietary restrictions or allergies.
We maintain a safe, background-verified, CPR & First Aid certified environment. Our space is fully childproofed, sanitized daily, and under constant supervision at all times.
We send daily reports with photos and notes so you always know how your child's day is going. We're also available by text or call during the day for any updates.
Start by scheduling a tour. After visiting, we'll provide an enrollment packet. Once completed, a deposit secures your child's spot. Space is limited due to our small group model.
Children with fever, vomiting, or contagious illness must stay home and may return 24 hours after symptoms fully resolve. This policy protects all children in our care.
Yes! We offer part-time options with 2 or 3 flexible days per week. Contact us to discuss a schedule that works for your family's needs.
